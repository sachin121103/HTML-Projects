Canteen's Chicken Fingers

The fabled Chicken Fingers was the subject of an episode in Season 1 entitled 'Contemporary American Poultry'. It is not only the most popular food in the community college, but an addiction. Abed and Jeff seize the means of production of chicken Fingers and starts a Italian-American Mafia-like syndicate to control its distribution and get favours for themselves for students and teachers alike. We will see how the chicken fingers are made below:

Ingredients

  • 1500 ml of Peanut Oil
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • Salt, to taste
  • 225 grams of All-Purpose Flour
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper
    • And most important of them all,
  • 1 package of uncooked chicken tenders

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet to 375 Fahrenheit or 190 Celsius
  2. In a large, shallow dish, combine salt and eggs
  3. In another dish, combine salt, black pepper and the All-Purpose flour
  4. Dredge the Chicken Tenders in the egg dish, and then coat in the flour. Do this process twice (Double Dredge)
  5. Fry the tenders for 5-8 minutes in the hot oil.
  6. Transfer the tenders to a baking sheet lined with paper towels and leave to cool for 10 minutes

The chicken fingers are ready to eat!
