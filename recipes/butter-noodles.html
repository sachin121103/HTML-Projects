Abed's Buttered Noodles

This is Abed's favourite food. He absolutely loves buttered Noodles and can eat it every day of the week. He first mentions Buttered noodles in Season 3 of the show. He even gets a box of 24 packets of buttered noodles for his birthday from Shirley. We will see how he makes his buttered noodles below.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Papa Penne's Egg Noodles
  • Butter, 2 tablespoons
  • Fresh Ground Pepper
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Add salt to a pot of water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Then, add in the pasta until is cooked al dente. This should be done in 7-8 minutes
  3. Put aside half a cup of the water and drain the pasta. Return the pot to the stove
  4. Add the noodles to the pot, then add the butter and pasta water to the pot. Then, add the salt
  5. Give it a stir until everything is dissolved and mixed in well. It's now ready to serve!
