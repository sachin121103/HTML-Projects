Shirley's Mini Pies

Shirley's favourite hobby is baking, and we see this in arguably the best episode in Community's history: 'Remedial Chaos Theory', featuring in Season 3 Episode 4. In the first and third timelines, her pies are succesfully baked. However, in the second timeline, the group lets her pies burn because they believe she has a baking addiction. Nonetheless, these are absolutely delicious pies that Shirley cooks with her heart. Lets see how it is made!

Ingredients

  • 2 pastries of 9-inch single crust pies
  • 3 cups diced apple
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/8 tablespoons of ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tablespoons of ground cinnamon
  • 1 package of uncooked chicken tenders
  • Lemon Juice
  • 2 tablespoons of instant tapioca

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit or 200 degrees Celsius
  2. Cut as many 6-inch rounds of pie crusts as possible, leaving some to cut into strips. Fit the rounds into pie moulds
  3. In a bowl, mix the cut apples, tapioca, sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon and nutmeg until thoroughly combined
  4. Allow the mixture to stand for 5-10 minutes
  5. Spoon the mixture into the pie shells and cover with the strips on top.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes and leave to cool before stirring
